Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to EduJoy Kids! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our educational app designed for children ages 3-10. We are committed to protecting your child's privacy and providing a safe, engaging learning environment.

Key Point: EduJoy Kids follows strict privacy guidelines to ensure a safe experience for children. We do not collect sensitive personal information, and parents maintain full control over their children's experience.

Information We Collect

EduJoy Kids is designed with children's privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from children
  • The app may collect anonymous usage data to improve our services
  • Any in-app progress or achievements are stored locally on the device
  • We use Facebook Ads and Google AdMob to display child-safe advertisements

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve the educational content and user experience
  • Provide appropriate, child-friendly advertisements
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Develop new educational features and games

Parental Controls

We believe parents should have control over their children's digital experience:

  • Parents can review the app content and features
  • No social features or external communication is enabled
  • Parents can disable in-app purchases (if any) through device settings
  • Parents can request deletion of any stored data by contacting us

Third-Party Services

EduJoy Kids uses the following third-party services that may collect anonymous information:

  • Facebook Ads: For displaying age-appropriate advertisements
  • Google AdMob: For serving child-safe ads within the app
  • Analytics tools: To understand how the app is used and improve our services

All third-party services comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and other relevant regulations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information collected through the app:

  • Data encryption for information in transit
  • Regular security reviews of our systems
  • Limited access to any collected data
  • Secure storage practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify parents of any material changes through the app or via email if contact information is provided. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
